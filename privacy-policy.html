Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Financial Add-ons collects, uses, stores, and protects Google user data when you use our Google Workspace Add-ons for Google Sheets.

Information We Access

Our Google Sheets Add-ons only access the following Google user data, and only with your explicit permission:

  • Google Sheets content (read-only access to the spreadsheets you explicitly select)
  • Your email address for licensing and identification purposes

How We Use Your Data

We access your Google Sheets data solely for the purpose of providing the Add-on functionality, such as retrieving and displaying financial data relevant to your sheets. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any other unrelated purposes.

Your email address is used only to validate your license and offer user support.

How We Share Your Data

We do not share, transfer, sell, or disclose your Google user data with any third party under any circumstances.

How We Protect Your Data

Your data is transmitted securely via Google's APIs. We do not store your spreadsheet content on our servers. Email addresses are stored securely in compliance with industry standards to ensure protection from unauthorized access.

Data Retention & Deletion

We do not retain any spreadsheet data. Your email address may be kept only as long as necessary to maintain your license or provide user support. You can request deletion of your email address from our records by contacting us at levyd01.addons@gmail.com.

Our website and Add-ons may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy policies or practices of any third parties.
