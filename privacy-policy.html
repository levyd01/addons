Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Financial Add-ons collects, uses, stores, and protects Google user data when you use our Google Workspace Add-ons for Google Sheets™.

Information We Access

Our Google Sheets™ Add-ons access the following Google user data, and only with your explicit permission:

  • Google Sheets™ content (read-only access to the spreadsheets you explicitly select)
  • Your email address for licensing and identification purposes

How We Use Your Data

We access your Google Sheets™ data solely to provide the Add-on’s functionality, such as retrieving and displaying financial data relevant to your spreadsheets. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any unrelated purposes.

Your email address is used only to validate your license and to provide user support.

How We Share Your Data

We do not share, transfer, sell, or disclose your Google user data to any third parties under any circumstances.

How We Protect Your Data

Your data is transmitted securely via Google’s APIs. We do not store your spreadsheet content on our servers. Email addresses are stored securely in compliance with industry standards to protect against unauthorized access.

Data Retention & Deletion

We do not retain any spreadsheet data. Your email address may be stored only as long as necessary to maintain your license or provide user support. You can request deletion of your email address from our records by contacting us at levyd01.addons@gmail.com.
